Devine Chemicals a leading Chemical company, is excited to announce a new strategic partnership with INEOS AGROCHEM, a renowned Agricultural and Chemical company. This collaboration aims to to create more financial stability to the society and reduce economical crisis in the wolrd.
Through this partnership, Devine Chemicals and INEOS GROUP will leverage their combined expertise and resources to expand product offerings, enhance customer experience, and create more safer ivestment platform.


Jim Devine, CEO of Devine Chemicals stated, “We are thrilled to partner with INEOS GROUP. Their innovative approach to technology complements our mission to create a more safer investment platform for the people and our customers. Together, we look forward to creating a safer, reliable, trusted and transparent investment platform as well as marketing our product DeCAL, DeTOX, DeLOUR across the Globe". Sir Jim Ratcliffe, Founder and Chairman of INEOS Group added, “Our collaboration with Devine Chemicals represents a significant step forward in Agricultural, Plasticsd, and Construction product supply. By combining our strengths, we are well-positioned to offer unmatched services to our customers and lead the investment market,".


This partnership is effective immediately, and both companies are committed to a seamless integration process to ensure the delivery of superior results to all stakeholders.
